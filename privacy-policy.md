# Privacy Policy for Record Keeper

**Effective Date:** 2026

## Overview

Record Keeper is designed with privacy as a core principle. This application does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data created and used within the app remains entirely on your device.

---

## Information Collection and Use

Record Keeper does **not collect any personal information** from users.

The app does not:

* Require account creation
* Track user activity
* Collect analytics data
* Use third-party services
* Make network requests of any kind

---

## Local Data Storage

All data entered into Record Keeper is stored **locally on your device only**. This may include:

* Album details (such as artist, title, year, etc.)
* Album cover images
* Tags and categorization data
* Saved searches and preferences

This data is **never transmitted** outside of your device by the application.

---

## Data Sharing

Record Keeper does **not share any data** with third parties.

---

## Data Security

Because all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted, its security depends on the security of your device. Users are encouraged to use device-level protections such as passcodes, biometrics, and encryption.

---

## Data Deletion

Users may delete their data at any time by:

* Removing individual records within the app, or
* Uninstalling the application, which removes all associated data

---

## Children’s Privacy

Record Keeper does not collect personal data and is safe for users of all ages.

---

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected in an updated version of this document with a revised effective date.
